Redis-3.0.4启动脚本

发表于 2016-03-05 | 分类于 Middleware

启动脚本在源码包目录下：
/root/backup/redis-3.0.4/utils/redis_init_script
配置文件在/root/backup/redis-3.0.4/redis.conf
拷贝到init.d下：
cp /root/backup/redis-3.0.4/utils/redis_init_script /etc/init.d/redis-server
修改安装于配置问价路径：

拷贝配置文件到安装目录：
cp /root/backup/redis-3.0.4/redis.conf /app/redis/6379.conf

改权限：chmod 755 redis-server
加到开机服务：
首先添加运行级别：

chkconfig –add redis-server
设置开机自启动：
chkconfig redis-server on

keepalived-1.2.19启动脚本

发表于 2016-03-05 | 分类于 Middleware

找到编译的安装目录，之际拷贝到的init.d
cp /app/keepalived/etc/rc.d/init.d/keepalived /etc/init.d/

修改vi /etc/init.d/keepalived

. /app/keepalived/etc/sysconfig/keepalived

PATH=”$PATH:/app/keepalived/sbin”

export PATH

修改vi /app/keepalived/etc/sysconfig/keepalived

KEEPALIVED_OPTIONS=”-D -f /app/keepalived/etc/keepalived/keepalived.conf”

改权限：
chmod 755 keepalived

加到开机服务：
chkconfig –add keepalived

设置开机自启动：
chkconfig rkeepalived on
